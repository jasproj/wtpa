Why Walk The Plank Adventures?

Captain Dane created Walk The Plank Adventures because he was tired of seeing corporate fishing charters with captains who didn't know the Keys and tourists who treated the water like an amusement park.

"I wanted to share the real Key West," Dane says. "Not the theme park version. The authentic experience — the fishing, the diving, the stories, the marine life, the sunsets that change your life."

Every charter with Walk The Plank Adventures is personal. You're not just booking a tour — you're getting direct access to a local legend who knows these waters better than anyone.
